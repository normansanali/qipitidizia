Mat Franco Biography

Mat Franco was born on 10 May 1988 and is 35 years old at the moment. He is a native of Johnston, Rhode Island, United States of America. He grew up alongside his two elder brothers.

When Mat was four years old, he saw magic on television and requested his parents to buy him a magic set. He subsequently began performing feats in his kindergarten class, and his grandmother, Eleanor Campellone, was a major inspiration and his first assistant. When he was too little to read for himself, she used to read magic instructions to him.

Similarly, one of his fondest childhood memories is of him and his grandmother attempting to figure out how to make cards appear out of thin air, similar to a magician they had seen on television. They hung a black curtain in the living room and cut a hole in it. His grandmother concealed herself on the opposite side and delivered cards to Mat through the curtain. His grandfather sat and watched Mat play with the excuse that “grandma must have stepped outside for a minute,” and he had no idea how the cards appeared.

As a child, he continued to study magic and performed whenever the opportunity presented itself. Mat had collected enough money from local events when he was 12 years old to fly to Las Vegas and study performance art with some of his idols, including Jeff McBride.

Mat Franco graduated from Johnston High School in 2006 and served as the wrestling team’s captain during his senior year. Then he attended the University of Rhode Island, earning a bachelor’s degree in business administration with a concentration in communication studies.
