Lucy Hales Dating History: David Henrie, Chris Zylka, Skeet Ulrich

Lucy Hale has been linked to actors, singers and various costars over the years.

“Every relationship I’ve had, I’ve learned something good and positive,” she told Nylon in December 2012. “We’re all just trying to figure it out. Some of us just have to do it publicly.”

The actress, who began her career by competing on American Juniors in 2003, and her star skyrocketed when she landed the role of Aria Montgomery on Pretty Little Liars, which premiered in 2010.

Hale is no stranger to catching feelings on the set of a series or movie. She told Entertainment Tonight in 2018 that it’s “absolutely” normal for costars to have crushes on each other, admitting that she’s had “lots of little showmances” in the past.
