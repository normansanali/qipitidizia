Priyanka Chopra Wears a Backless Sheer Dress on Date Night with Nick Jonas

Priyanka Chopra and Nick Jonas are spending some quality time together in New York City.

Earlier this week, the couple headed out for a date night, each taking the opportunity to show off their individual styles.

The Quantico star cut a fashionable figure in a head-to-toe black look. The centerpiece of her outfit was a black maxi dress, which featured a basque waistline, sheer long sleeves, and criss-crossing straps on the open back. Chopra paired the dress with towering black heeled pumps. Other accessories included a black mini handbag with a gold chain handle and large hoop earrings.

Meanwhile, Jonas looked suave in a silky button-up with a red, white, blue, and yellow pattern all over. He wore the statement shirt with satin navy blue pants and white sneakers.

The couple—who recently welcomed the arrival of their first daughter, Malti—announced that they're going into business together earlier this year. The two became strategic investors and advisers to Perfect Moment, a luxury ski and surf fashion brand.

"I came to the quick conclusion that I'm more an après-ski girl, rather than skiing. … I bought a lot of clothes that were winter chic. A lot of them happened to be Perfect Moment," Chopra told Variety. "Last year, I was filming my new show Citadel in London. Nick and I met [Perfect Moment owners] Jane and Max Gottschalk and talked about our love for the brand. They talked about growing the brand and we all had an 'aha' moment and decided to invest and become partners."
