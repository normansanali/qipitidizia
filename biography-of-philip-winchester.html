Philip Winchester is an American actor widely known for playing the role of Peter Stone in Justice and Chicago Law & Order: SVU.

He has been married to Megan Coughlin since 2008. The two are parents to two children, one of whom they welcomed this year.

Born in Belgrade, Montana, LAMDA Graduate

Philip Winchester was born Philip C. Winchester on March 24, 1981 in Belgrade, Montana, United States of America, to Jay Winchester and Barbara Winchester .

Later, he and his family moved to London, UK, which is his mother’s native country. He is of American nationality and belongs to the white ethnic group.

Regarding his academic background, Winchester attended Belgrade High School in Belgrade, Montana, from where he graduated in 1999.

He later joined the London Academy of Music and Dramatic Art (LAMDA) where he studied acting for three years.

Film and Television Career

Philip Winchester began his acting career with a minor role in the action thriller The Patriot in 1998. Steven Seagal and Camilla Belle were the main cast of the film.

In 2003, he appeared in the independent film, The Telephone , which was produced by Robert Finlay in Bristol.

Later in 2008, he played the role of Robinson Crusoe in the NBC action television series Crusoe . His film credits include In My Dream and Solomon Kane . He has also appeared in the Syfy TV miniseries, Alicia .

In 2010, he portrayed Frank Stanton , the fiancé of Olivia Dunham (played by Anna Torv ), on the popular Fox television series, Fringe .

His other film and television credits are Chicago Justice, Law & Order: SVU, The Hi-Line , The Patriot , LD 50 Lethal Dose , King Lear , Thunderbirds , CSI Miami , Strike Back , In My Dream , Flying Boys , The heart of the earth and Shaking the land of dreams .

Estimated net worth of $5 million

Philip Winchester, being a stunning and talented actress, earns a substantial amount of money from his movies and television.

According to the Information Cradle website, Winchester has an estimated net worth of $5 million. As for her salary, the 38-year-old Law & Order: SVU star was earning between $20 and $30 thousand per episode of Strip .

Married to Megan Coughlin, father of two children

Talking about Philip Winchester’s marital status, as we mentioned earlier, the Chicago Justice actor has been married to Megan Coughlin.

The two met in their hometown of Belgrade, Montana, and eventually began dating. The groom-bride then walked down the aisle in an intimate ceremony in 2008.

The couple welcomed their first child; daughter Charlie Winchester in 2015. The husband-and-wife duo welcomed their second child, a baby girl, on February 14, 2019, just one day after they announced the pregnancy news.
