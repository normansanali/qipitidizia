Gordon Peter Getty Net Worth

Gordon Peter Getty is an American philanthropist and businessman who was born on December 20, 1933 in California. He is best known for selling his family's Getty Oil to Texaco for $10 billion in 1983, making him the richest person in America with a net worth of $2 billion that same year. He has since become a major philanthropist, donating to various causes and organizations.
