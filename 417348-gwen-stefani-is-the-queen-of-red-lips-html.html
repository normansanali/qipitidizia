Gwen Stefani Is the Queen of Red Lips: How She Would Look If She Wore Natural Makeup

  • Gwen Stefani is known for her iconic red lipstick and platinum blond hair.
  • The singer has never been shy about wearing bold makeup, but some fans thought she looked younger with softer colors.
  • Here are a few AI-generated photos that show how Stefani would have looked with subtle makeup.

Gwen Stefani broke into fame as the lead vocalist of the band 'No Doubt," but throughout her career, she has proven to be a multifaceted entertainer and businesswoman. She is loved as one of the judges on "The Voice," but she's also tapped into her passion for makeup and started her makeup brand, GXVE Beauty.

Gwen Stefani attends at the "Mayor of the Sunset Strip" premiere in Los Angeles in 2003 | Source: Getty Images

Starting a makeup brand is not a complete surprise because one of Stefani's signature looks is a bold red lipstick, which she has rocked for decades. However, many fans might not have known that the singer used to work at a makeup counter at an AR local department store in Anaheim, California, in the late 1980s.

She developed her makeup skills by voluntarily doing people's makeup as a free gift for their purchase. "I was a fake makeup artist in the sense that they never trained me, and I didn't ever report to anybody," she reiterated.

Gwen Stefani photographed in 1999 | Source: Getty Images

The "Voice" judge said she loved the confidence that came out of people every time they saw themselves. That feeling they embraced was something she carried with her forever.

As a celebrity, makeup is a big part of Stefani's life, but getting her "glam" done is one of her favorite processes. Over the years, the singer has developed a passion for makeup and even said it was something she would speak about all day if she could.

Gwen Stefani and Gavin Rossdale at the Radio Music Awards in Las Vegas in 2000 | Source: Getty Images

Stefani has built her signature look behind her platinum blonde hair and bright red lip color, it is no surprise that her makeup line includes different kinds and shades of red lipstick.

However, some of her fans are of the opinion that she would look much younger if she wore more neutral colors. Once, a Facebook user said, "She looks a lot younger with softer colors!" while another added, "So much prettier and softer if she ditched the ruby red lips!"

Gwen Stefani at the Grammy Awards in 2001 | Source: Getty Images

Another commenter said, "Honestly. Personally, I think Gwen looks beautiful as well as Younger the LESS makeup she wears." In 2016, Stefani shared a picture of herself without any makeup, and internet users could not stop complimenting her beauty.

Gwen Stefani's Beauty Secrets

Despite how fans might feel about Stefani's bold makeup looks, the singer always makes sure she takes care of her skin and has been able to maintain a flawless complexion.

Gwen Stefani at VH-1 Music Awards in 2001 | Source: Getty Images

One of her secrets to clear and youthful skin is not sleeping with makeup on. The singer said she learned the repercussions of not washing her face before bed early on. "At bedtime, I always take off my makeup and put moisturizer on," she added.

Another tip is that she keeps her skincare routine simple. Stefani is aware of the overwhelming variety of skincare products, but using minimal products has helped her skin.

Since she's getting older, the 54-year-old also prioritizes keeping her skin moisturized. One of her favorite products to get glowy skin is from her makeup line. Stefani said it is a face oil that gives her the best youthful glow.

Gwen Stefani at the MTV Video Music Awards in 2001 | Source: Getty Images

She also never makes the mistake of forgetting to apply sunscreen. "I would tell my younger self to stay out of the sun and wear sunscreen," said the mother of three.

Stefani recalled working as a lifeguard and wishing she knew about the importance of applying sun protection. Fortunately, she was still able to save her skin from sun damage when she stopped sunbathing in her 20s.

Last but not least, Stefani's tip for keeping her skin glowing is staying consistent with her skincare routine. But even her makeup depends on how her skin looks and feels.

The "Rich Girl singer admitted that some skin days are better than others, but when preparing for a shoot, she will use foundation or concealer with a bit more coverage. When she's with her family, her routine is kept very simple. Stefani's love for makeup is all about exuding the most confidence while also making sure her skin remains healthy.
