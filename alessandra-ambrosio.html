Alessandra Ambrosio - Biography

The Victoria's Secret Angel and model for houses including Gucci, Dior and Armani was born in 1981

    Early life

    Alessandra Corine Ambrosio was born on 11 April 1981, in the small town of Erexim in Southern Brazil to petrol station-owner parents. The future supermodel who would become one of several Brazilian models to hit the international scene big time in the late 1990s developed her passion for the fashion business at an early age, enrolling in modelling classes at the age of 12.

    Modelling success

    In 1996 the stunning brunette entered Elite model agency's Look Of The Year competition and, although she didn't win, was soon listed on its books. After appearing on the cover of Brazilian , she made the leap to ad campaigns for Guess.
