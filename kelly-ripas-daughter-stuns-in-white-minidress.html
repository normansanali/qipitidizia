Kelly Ripa's Daughter Stuns In White Minidress

Kelly Ripa‘s daughter Lola Consuelos was all smiles as she stunned in a white minidress and had fans gushing over her being “the graduate” last year. Looking gorgeous in May 2023 photos, the 22-year-old showed off her figure in a stylish minidress while marking the end of her NYU graduation, and fans have made sure the snaps stay live. Flaunting her curves and looking super stylish, the daughter to Live! hosts Kelly Ripa and Mark Consuelos posed with her academic cap in one shot, although the fan account sharing the snaps made sure to also include 52-year-old mom Kelly, plus the star’s hubby Mark.

All Smiles In Her Graduation Minidress

Showing off her shapely arms and waistline, the “Divine Timing” singer held her cap alongside a black box in one hand, also slinging a purple coat over her shoulder as she sported her dark locks down.

Breaking Into Music

In 2022, Lola broke into the music biz with the release of her “Paranoia Silverlining” single. In 2023, she dropped “Divine Timing.”

Of her parents being the first to listen to her beats, Lola revealed: “My parents are the last people to sugarcoat anything when it comes to my singing. They are going to tell me if it sounds bad! So when I saw they had a good reaction and liked it, I felt reassured.”

“My parents, oddly, even though they’re in the business, the one thing they have actually no idea about is music,” she continued. “They really don’t know what they’re talking about once when it comes to music. So it’s very sweet to see them do their posts here and there and their little videos.”
