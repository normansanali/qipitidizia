Fanatics Fest NYC 2024: A Spectacle for Sports Fans, Culture Enthusiasts, and Collectors

Fanatics Fest NYC will bring together fans of all ages for an unprecedented 3-day celebratory experience at the Javits Center in New York City on August 16-18, 2024.

The groundbreaking event will unite sports fans, culture aficionados, and collectors alike with landmark experiences like meeting sports legends, and sports card buying selling and trading. 

Not only that but ultra rare memorabilia, and shopping limited-edition merch featuring exclusive drops. Here we get into what makes Fanatics Fest NYC 2024 a must-see event for sports fans and collectors everywhere.


Unforgettable Experiences

Live Panels - Photo Ops - Autographs
The ultimate fan experience for sports and celebrity enthusiasts.

Fanatics Fest NYC will showcase exclusive panels, photo ops, and autograph signings, allowing fans to meet some of the biggest names in sports and entertainment including Tom Brady, Derek Jeter, Kevin Durant, Sabrina Ionescu and many more.

This will be a once-in-a-lifetime experience in which fans will have the chance to snap pics with their favorite athletes and legends.

League and Brand Immersion

Fanatics Fest NYC will feature top leagues & sports with the most innovative brands in sports featuring NFL, NBA, WNBA, UFC, WWE, MLB, NHL, MLS, and more, bringing fans closer than ever before to their favorite sports.

  • Step into the world of top sports leagues such as NFL, NBA, WNBA, UFC, WWE, MLB, NHL, MLS, and more through immersive activations and exhibits.
  • Embrace the thrill of the court, the ring, or the field, and showcase your athleticism in a truly immersive environment.

Fanatics Superstore 

  • The Fanatics Superstore offers a wide array of official sports apparel from all the major leagues, teams, and players.
  • Visitors can indulge in a diverse selection of gear from NFL, MLB, NBA, NHL, NCAA, and more, including exclusive apparel collaborations with top names and brands.

Lifestyle Zone

  • The Lifestyle Zone is a haven for sports and pop culture enthusiasts, offering exclusive access to merchandise and product drops from the hottest brands.
  • Whether you're a sneakerhead, trendsetter, or simply a lover of all things cool, this zone is a celebration of the dynamic synergy between sports and pop culture.

Collector Workshop Stage 

  • An interactive space where collectors of all levels can learn and experience card collecting through workshops led by industry experts.
  • Workshops cover a range of topics, from Collecting 101 to How to Turn Your Hobby into a Business, catering to diverse interests within the world of collecting.


Fanatics Emporium and Life Style Area

The Fanatics Superstore features a category-killing assortment of official sports merchandise across all the major leagues, while the Lifestyle Zone offers unique merchandise and special product drops from the hottest brands.

This will create an immersive experience that celebrates the convergence of sports and pop culture.


Kids Zone and The Memorabilia Museum

Here will be a wide range of time and opportunity for young sports fans to put their passion on the field.

Embracing 50 years of sports history, the Memorabilia Museum will grant entry to a worn & unsigned jersey as this collection talks a thousand victory or defeat stories.

  • The ultimate playground for young sports fans, offering a variety of interactive games and challenges.
  • From mini-basketball hoops to soccer dribbling challenges, the Kids Zone promises endless fun and excitement for our youngest sports enthusiasts.
This haven for sports aficionados and collectors features vintage jerseys, signed memorabilia, and artifacts that tell compelling stories of triumph, perseverance, and the spirit of competition.


Pricing and Promotions

Fanatics Fest NYC tickets cost between $20 and $400, while General Admission Adult tickets run $50 each day.

VIP includes all three days of the fest and access to exclusive product drops with private, fast-lane entry -- the way to go if you consider yourself a "huge" fan.

Special Accommodations

Fanatics Fest NYC will promote a fun and inclusive environment to all attendees and have access to ramps for the stages and separate ADA lines for comfort and security.

There are service animals for guests with disabilities, and electric scooters and manually operated wheelchairs are available for rent at the venue, making sure that everyone is accommodated.


To sum it up, Fanatics Fest 2024 is shaping up to be a unique cultural event for sports fans, pop culture aficionados, and collectors.

Boasting an unprecedented slate of experiences that range from live panels and autograph signings to immersive brand activations and exclusive merch drops, this event will undoubtedly be a must-stop destination for fans of all ages.

The three-day event will take place on August 16-18, 2024 so save the date and take part in the making of sports history with Fanatics Fest NYC!
