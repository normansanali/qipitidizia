Chet Hanks Biography – Chet Hanks Wiki

Chet Hanks born Chester Marlon Hanks is an American actor. According to his IMDb profile, he is known for Indiana Jones and the Kingdom of the Crystal Skull (2008), Larry Crowne (2011) and Project X (2012). He has had recurring roles on the television shows Maron, Shameless, and Empire. He also appeared in two episodes of Curb Your Enthusiasm. In November 2019, Deadline revealed that Chet was set to recur on a new show for Showtime, called Your Honor.

Chet Hanks Age

Chet Hanks was born on August 4, 1990, in Los Angeles, California, United States.

Chet Hanks Parents, Family

Chet is the son of actor Tom Hanks and actress Rita Wilson. He has an elder brother, Truman Theodore Hanks, who was born on December 26, 1995. He also has 2 half-brothers, Colin Hanks and Elizabeth Hanks.

Chet Hanks Wife – Chet Hanks Baby Mama

Chet Hanks’ baby mama is Tiffany Miles.

Chet Hanks Daughter

Chet has a daughter, Michaiah, who was born in 2016. In December 2017, Chet shared a video of him reading a book to Michaiah as she sits in his arms. He wrote in the caption, “The best part of my year. The person who saved my life.”

In an interview with Entertainment Tonight, Chet said that both Tom and Rita are wonderful grandparents to his daughter: “It’s awesome seeing them being grandparents as well because I was really close with my grandparents and now my daughter gets to have the same experience.”
