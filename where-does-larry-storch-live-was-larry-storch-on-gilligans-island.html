Where does Larry Storch live? Was Larry Storch on Gilligans Island?

Larry Storch is a renowned American actor and comedian, best known for his comic television roles and voice-over works.

Lawrence Samuel Storch was born on January 8, 1923, in New York, New York, the United States to Alfred Storch, a realtor, and his wife, Sally Kupperman Storch, a telephone operator.

Larry Storch had a younger brother, Jay who was an actor/voiceover performer under the name Jay Lawrence. He attended DeWitt Clinton High School, however, he never graduated from high school.

Larry Storch worked as a stand-up comic for a $12 a week opening for bandleader Al Donahue in Sheepshead Bay. He served in the United States Navy during World War II.

Larry Storch was recognized for his excellent voice-over work for cartoon shows such as Mr. Whoopee on Tennessee Tuxedo and His Tales, as well as his live-action role of the bumbling Corporal Randolph Agarn on F Troop.

Larry Storch had married actress Norma Catherine Greve on July 10, 1961, and remained married until her death at age 81 on August 28, 2003. Larry is the father of a stepson named Lary May; a daughter, Candace Herman, and a stepdaughter, June Cross.

Where does Larry Storch live?

Larry Storch lives in the Upper West Side of New York, the United States.

Was Larry Storch on Gilligan’s Island?

Yes, Larry Storch appeared in Gilligan’s Island” Little Island, Big Gun (TV Episode 1965) as Jackson Farrell.
