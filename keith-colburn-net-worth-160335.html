About

Owner and captain of the FV Wizard and reality star on the Discovery Channel's hit series Deadliest Catch. He also starred on After the Catch, a mini-series that follows the captains and crew from the popular reality show when they're not filming.

Before Fame

Prior to becoming a reality TV star, he worked as a commercial crab Fisherman as far back as 1985.

Trivia

He is also an Entrepreneur and cook. He has been working in French restaurants since he was 14, before he eventually became an executive chef. He has his own product line of sauces and dry rubs called Captain Keith's Catch, which hit grocery shelves in 2015.

Family Life

He married Florence Colburn, and he has two children, Caelan and Sienna.

Associated With

On Deadliest Catch, he starred alongside brothers Edgar and Sig Hansen.
