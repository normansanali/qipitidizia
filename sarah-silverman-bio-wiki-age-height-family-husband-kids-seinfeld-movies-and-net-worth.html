    Sarah Silverman Seinfeld

    Silverman took some time during this week’s episode of The Sarah Silverman Podcast to remember the bad experience she had to work with comedian Michael Richards during her 1997 guest appearance on Seinfeld. While the two performers became sociable later in life, their relationship began under nervous occasions on the set of the NBC comedy series after Silverman failed one of her lines. The script read, “It’s probably the wind,” but Silverman said, “It’s probably the rain.”

    “This guy, Michael Richards, breaks character and just starts ripping me a new asshole,” Silverman said. “He points to the window and he goes, ‘Do you see rain in that window? Do you see rain in that window?’ and I go, ‘No,’ and he says, ‘Then why did you say rain?’ It’s not raining. There’s no rain in that window! The line is ‘wind’!”
