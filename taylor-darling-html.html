ASMR Darling - Age, Family, Bio

Content creator on YouTube who specializes in ASMR videos on her channel. She has garnered more than 2.5 million subscribers. Many of her videos have been meant to help the listeners fall asleep such as her "ASMR 20 Triggers To Help You Sleep" video which has received over 37 million views. 

Before Fame

She created her YouTube channel in December of 2014, but did not publish her first ASMR video until "ASMR Whispered Bubbly Makeup Roleplay."

Trivia

She did theater growing up and played sports including soccer, tennis, volleyball and flag football. She won homecoming lady her freshman year of high school. In February of 2017, she posted an ASMR video of herself whispering an explanation of what ASMR is. 

Family Life

Her name is Taylor Darling. She is from Florida and has a sister and a brother. She frequently features her dog Arya on her channel.

Associated With

She was featured in a video by PewDiePie called "My Favorite Horror Game."
