Dana Brooke Breaks Silence After TNA Wrestling Hard to Kill Debut

Dana Brooke was released from WWE, but she was one of 2 fired ex Superstars who showed up at TNA Wrestling Hard to Kill. Now, she is making a statement.

Dolph Ziggler also showed up at TNA Wrestling‘s Hard to Kill event. That was a bit of a shock, as the company continues in their re-branding efforts.

While dropping new glamour shot on Instagram, she only included her new ring name as the caption for her post. Dana Brooke is now Ashe by Elegance, and that is quite a name and a statement at the same time.

“Ash by Elegance”

We will have to see what Dana Brooke does in TNA Wrestling. It looks like she is certainly presenting a brand-new kind of character for her, which will extend her acting chops a bit. Only time will tell if she pulls it off, but she has a lot of fans’ attention.

WWE never gave Dana Brooke a lot, but she was down for whatever. Now, she has another chance in TNA Wrestling, and she is already entering the situation with a spotlight on her.

Dana Brooke was never discredited for her work ethic in WWE, nor how much she put into becoming the best pro wrestler she could be. Sadly, her time with the company came to a sudden end, just as she was starting to gain momentum in NXT.
