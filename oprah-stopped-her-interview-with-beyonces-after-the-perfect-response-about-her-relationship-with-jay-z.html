Beyoncé Revealed That She And Jay-Z Always Did Their Best To Keep The Relationship Out Of The Spotlight

When they were dating and got married, nothing changed for both Jay-Z and Beyoncé when it came to exposing their relationship to the public. Both sides have always kept things quiet, and in turn, this was a major part of their success as a couple.

"Jay and I have always been very discreet when it comes to our relationship. Even after we got married nothing changed about that. I think that saved us a lot of trouble."

Beyoncé would go on to admit that she never felt pressure to get married and in fact, it was very much the opposite. Beyoncé wanted to take her time and make sure she was ready for the next step.

“No one expected me to run off and get married. I really don’t believe that you will love the same thing when you’re 20 as you do at 30. So that was my rule: Before the age of 25, I would never get married. I feel like you have to get to know yourself, know what you want, spend some time by yourself, and be proud of who you are before you can share that with someone else."

Beyoncé truly has a refreshing take on marriage, and that held true during her interview with Oprah. A particular line received lots of applause from both the host and audience.

Beyoncé Received Praise By Oprah After She Revealed Her Independence Comes Before Being A Wife

As expected, Oprah asked Beyoncé about her relationship to Jay-Z at the time. Oprah wondered if the label of wife felt better than girlfriend. However, the artist has a different mindset when it comes to the label. Her response received a huge reaction from both the host and fans on-hand for the interview.

When asked about being a wife over a girlfriend, Beyonce said, "It feels great, but you can have your own life before your someone else's wife." Oprah responds, "Yes," while extending her hand.

The two continue on these days with their same principles, keeping the relationship quiet, while Beyoncé herself rarely appears in interviews. However, she is giving others advice and feedback relating to interviews with Oprah, including Meghan Markle.

Beyoncé texted to check in, while Markle was just shocked she knew about her.

Markle reacted to the moment with joy, "She said she wants me to feel safe and protected. She admires and respects my bravery and vulnerability and thinks I was selected to break generational curses that need to be healed."
