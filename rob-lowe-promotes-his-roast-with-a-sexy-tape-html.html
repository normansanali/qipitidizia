Rob Lowe Promotes His Comedy Central Roast With a Sex Tape of Sorts

Rob Lowe has lived through so many scandals in his career and come out even more handsome and successful on the other end he should teach a class on surviving Hollywood. If you could major in Rob Lowe at the University of Southern California, somewhere around the 300-level courses you would get a frame-by-frame breakdown of the new trailer for his Comedy Central Roast. It’s a tiny little seminar in self-aware mockery, and the perfect nod to not only his hit movie, St. Elmo’s Fire, but also to his real sex-tape scandal from 1988. The roast will air on Monday, September 5 at 10 p.m.; tune in to see Lowe get razzed by roast master David Spade and guests like Peyton Manning, Rob Riggle, Ralph Macchio, Nikki Glaser, and of course, Jewel and Ann Coulter.
