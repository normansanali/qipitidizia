Charlize Theron Glowed Like a Goddess in a Bejeweled Sheer Dress and Knee-High Gladiator Sandals

In case you haven't been keeping up with the events of fashion month, Charlize Theron is on a very stylish tour de force around Paris, and it’s safe to say her stellar looks are showing no signs of slowing down.

On Tuesday, after arriving at Dior’s photo call in a completely sheer beaded maxiskirt, the actress later upped the ante in an even more jaw-dropping number for the fashion house’s spring-summer 2024 Paris Fashion Week show. For the occasion, she slipped into a totally see-through, bejeweled frock from the fashion house with nothing but an itty-bitty cream bralette and a coordinating micro-miniskirt underneath. The actress further committed to the goddess aesthetic with a pair of metallic gold, knee-high gladiator sandals. Pearl drop earrings and a black clutch provided the finishing touches to her neoclassical look.

When it came to the beauty department, she styled her blonde bob in effortless waves and a middle part and added a light pink eyeshadow with a generous swirl of blush and glossy pink lips.

Earlier that day, Charlize stuck to the same beaded theme at the label’s photo call, but this time, she unexpectedly paired her chainmail maxskirt with a crisp white button-up. She layered the skimming piece over a pair of black underwear and accessorized with a black clutch, matching sandals, and gold letter rings that spelled out “Dior." Her slicked-back ponytail also allowed a pair of pearl earrings to peek out.
