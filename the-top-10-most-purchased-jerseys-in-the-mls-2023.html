The Top 10 Most Purchased Jerseys in the MLS 2023

The MLS (Major League Soccer) has become increasingly popular over the years, and one of the most sought-after items for fans is the players' jerseys. These jerseys not only represent the teams and players but also serve as a way for fans to show their support and loyalty. In 2023, MLS released the top 25-selling jerseys, and here are the top 10 most purchased jerseys in the MLS for 2023 based on retail sales of Adidas MLS jerseys on MLSstore.com from January 1 through September 12, 2023.

  • Carlos Vela – LAFC: Carlos Vela, the Mexican superstar, has consistently been one of the top-selling players in MLS. His skill and popularity have made his jersey the best-selling in the league for 2023, showcasing his immense fan base and support.

  • Hany Mukhtar – Nashville SC: Hany Mukhtar's jersey comes in at the second spot, highlighting his impact and influence on the Nashville SC fanbase. The German midfielder has captured the hearts of fans with his performances on the field, leading to high jersey sales.

  • Walker Zimmerman – Nashville SC: Another Nashville SC player, Walker Zimmerman, secures the third spot in the top 10 most purchased jerseys. His solid presence in defense and contribution to the team's success have made him a favorite among the fans.

  • Javier "Chicharito" Hernández – LA Galaxy: The charismatic Mexican striker, known as "Chicharito," continues to draw immense support from fans, as reflected by the high sales of his jersey, placing him in the fourth position.

  • Jordan Morris – Seattle Sounders: Jordan Morris, the talented forward for Seattle Sounders, has garnered significant attention, leading to a strong demand for his jersey, making it one of the top 10 most purchased jerseys in the MLS for 2023.

  • Lionel Messi – Inter Miami CF: The global football icon's move to MLS has caused a surge in jersey sales, with his Inter Miami CF jersey being the highest-selling adidas jersey in the league, securing him the sixth spot in the top 10 list.

  • João Klauss – St. Louis CITY SC: João Klauss, the Brazilian forward, has made a notable impact in the MLS, reflected in the sales of his jersey, placing him in the seventh position in the top 10 most purchased jerseys for 2023.

  • João Paulo – Seattle Sounders: João Paulo's contribution to the Seattle Sounders has not gone unnoticed, as his jersey sales have earned him the eighth spot in the top 10 list, solidifying his popularity among fans.

  • Jakob Glesnes – Philadelphia Union: Jakob Glesnes, the Norwegian defender, has garnered significant support from Philadelphia Union fans, leading to his jersey being among the top 10 most purchased in the MLS for 2023.

  • Johnny Russell – Sporting KC: Johnny Russell's jersey rounds up the top 10 most purchased jerseys in the MLS for 2023, showcasing his influence and popularity among Sporting KC supporters.

    • These top 10 most purchased jerseys in the MLS for 2023 represent a diverse mix of players from different teams, reflecting the global appeal of the league and the influence of international stars like Lionel Messi alongside homegrown talent. The rankings are a testament to the players' impact on and off the field, as well as the unwavering support of the fans, who proudly wear their favorite players' jerseys, showcasing their allegiance and passion for the sport.

    The MLS has seen a surge in jersey sales, with players like Carlos Vela, Hany Mukhtar, and Walker Zimmerman leading the pack. The popularity of these players transcends borders, reflecting the league's growing prominence and appeal on the global stage. As the MLS continues to attract top talent and expand its reach, the demand for player jerseys is expected to rise, further solidifying the league's position in the world of football.
