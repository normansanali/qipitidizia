The Bar Pay Tribute to Bird Box During WWE Live Event Entrance

Originally published on ProWrestlingSheet.

Cesaro and Sheamus kinda-sorta took the Bird Box challenge this weekend during their entrance at a WWE live event.

Sheamus shared photos of The Bar's entrance at a live event in Fayetteville showing the duo wearing blindfolds like Sandra Bullock's character in the popular Netflix movie. 

For those unaware, the Bird Box challenge is a thing being done by YouTubers in which they go around wearing a blindfold for a certain period of time. This led to Netflix putting out a public notice telling people to not hurt themselves taking part in the challenge. 

Enjoy the pictures (and don't actually take part in one of these challenges at home or in public)!
