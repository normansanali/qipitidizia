Highlights

  • Brad Pitt is reportedly having his girlfriend Ines sign an NDA to protect his privacy and ensure her intentions are genuine.
  • Ines appears understanding of Brad's request, but has not completely moved in with him despite their increasingly serious relationship.
  • Brad's past marriage with Angelina Jolie had complications, including custody battles and allegations of excessive drinking and anger issues.

As Brad Pitt reportedly grows closer to his girlfriend Ines De Ramon, he’s apparently taking steps to protect himself, so he won’t get hurt like he did in his last long-term relationship with Angelina Jolie.

Speaking to In Touch Weekly, a source claimed that Brad is insistent Ines sign a non-disclosure agreement to prove she has the right intentions as well as to safeguard his privacy.

“It’s just that he values his privacy,” the insider explained. “People of Brad’s status don’t want their texts or anything personal shared with the universe. Brad wants transparency and honesty.”

Ines Allegedly Isn’t Surprised Brad Wants Her To Sign An NDA

Despite Brad’s demand, Ines is reportedly understanding of his request, as the source implies she’s open to signing the agreement. “[She] would have been surprised if he didn’t ask her to sign one,” the source went on.

"It's pretty recent," the insider said of their latest living arrangement, adding that Ines has not given up her own place completely.

Brad may still have reservations about being in a serious relationship, but that hasn’t stopped him from making a big move with Ines – literally. She’s apparently been staying at his home more frequently as they transition to living together.

"It's pretty recent," a source told PEOPLE. "They are going very strong and she is happier than ever."

Brad and Ines were first linked in 2022, and though they’ve remained relatively private about their relationship, its seemingly continued to get serious. Various reports claim the couple is looking forward to marriage and children.

Ines Reportedly Hasn’t Met Brad’s Kids Yet

Brad and Ines haven’t replied to the recent reports about the status of their romance, and neither has his ex-wife Angelina Jolie, who recently debuted a new blonde hairstyle in New York, where she’s been focusing on her fashion house Atelier Jolie.

Angelina Jolie and Brad Pitt were in a relationship for over a decade, from 2005 to 2016. They got married in 2014 but split within two years after Jolie filed, citing irreconcilable differences. They have three adopted children: Maddox (born 2001), Pax (born 2003), Zahara (born 2005), and three biological children: Shiloh (born 2006), and twins Knox and Vivienne (born 2008).
