2009 Burger King promotional Pokmon toys

From Bulbapedia, the community-driven Pokémon encyclopedia.

A checklist showcasing all available TCG cards.

The 2009 Burger King promotional Pokémon toys are a set of toys which were distributed with Kids Meals at Burger King restaurants from June 1 to 22, 2009. Every toy was paired with two reverse holofoil Platinum TCG cards (including a special Platinum stamp in the bottom right corner). There are six toys in total, including lenticular rockers, card holders, and card frames.
