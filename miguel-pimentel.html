Career

Miguel’s career is as a singer and actor. He secured a production deal with ‘Drop Squad’ in 2000. Likewise, he was signed to the independent record label Black Ice in 2004. He completed his debut studio album, ‘Young & Free,’ in 2006. ‘Black Ice’ delayed the release of the album, opting instead to release the song “Getcha Hands Up.” He left the label after being dissatisfied with the track, its video, and his overall treatment.

Miguel has since remarked that this experience taught him to take ownership of his marketing rather than to rely on the label to set him apart from the pack of male R&B performers. He acquired a new recording contract with ‘Jive Records’ in 2007.

Similarly, his follow-up album, ‘All I Want Is You,’ was delayed for several years due to a lawsuit filed by Black Ice for contract breach. Additionally, the album debuted in November 2010. All I Want Is You ended up spending 45 weeks on the Billboard charts and selling over 400,000 copies in the United States.

He has also dabbled with film. The films are titled ‘Live by Night’ (2016). He played Esteban Suarez in that film. He also starred as Malcolm in ‘Detroit’ (2017). Similarly, he starred as Himself in the 2017 television series ‘The Platinum Life.’ Additionally, he voiced Arcannis in the upcoming film ‘Fearless’ (2020).

He joined with Jive Records in 2007 and recorded his self-titled first album All I Want Is You. Its release was delayed for three years after Black Ice sued him for contract breach. When his legal difficulties were resolved, he released a mixtape and created songs for other Jive singers, including Usher, Musiq Soulchild, and Asher Roth.

Finally, in 2010, the lawsuit was settled, and All I Want Is You was released in November 2010. Regrettably, it was not as successful as he had intended. Although its title tune received some attention, it was enough to secure him a position as a support act on Trey Songz’s and Usher’s tours.

And respectively, the follow-up singles “Sure Thing” and “Quickie” peaked at No. 1 and No. 3 on the Billboard R&B/Hip-Hop Singles chart. Additionally, the chart-topping single “Adorn” received widespread radio and video play. For its seductive tone and smooth pace, “Adorn” has been compared to Marvin Gaye’s “Sexual Healing.”

Additionally, he won the ‘Soul Train Music Award for best R&B/soul male artist and was nominated for several other awards during the season. Additionally, he published his follow-up album ‘Wildheart’ in 2015. He later released ‘War & Leisure’ in 2017. It includes hit songs such as “Sky Walker, “By Rick Ross, and Travis Scott.
