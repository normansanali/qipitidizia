Natasha Liu Bordizzo (born on August 25, 1994, age:28) is a successful Australian model and Actress, who has been gaining fame since taking her first step in the Hollywood industry by working in the American-Chinese wuxia film “Crouching Tiger, Hidden Dragon: Sword of Destiny,” in which Director Woo-Ping Yuen gave her the chance to play the role of “Snow Vase.”

Since her beginning in Hollywood until 2022, she has worked on many projects, including Director J.J. Perry’s latest film, “Day Shift,” the movie is about a secret vampire hunter played by Jamie Foxx who just wants a good life for his daughter. Bordizzo here plays the character of Heather, a new friendly neighbor who gets looped up in the whole adventure.

Undoubtedly in 2022, she has been gaining attention by working with Jamie Foxx, but Liu’s prominence is steadily growing because Bordizzo nowadays has been working with Director Dave Filoni in his forthcoming star wars mini-series “Ahsoka,” in which Natasha will play “Sabine Wren,” a human female Mandalorian fighter.

Who are Natasha Liu Bordizzo’s parents?

Natasha Liu Bordizzo (28) was born on August 25, 1994, in Sydney City of Australia. Little is known about her parents; her mother comes from Chinese heritage, her father has an Italian background, and she is their only child.

Bordizzo comes from an academic background. She completed her schooling at Sydney Girls High School. She is not only intelligent academically but is also quite good in sports. At a very young age, she took up martial arts and trained in it for five years. In 2009, she earned a black belt in Taekwondo and learned Kenpō karate.

Then, at the age of 19, she went on to pursue a double degree in Bachelor of Law | Bachelor of Communication at the University of Technology Sydney. It was then she landed her first acting gig. Thanks to her fluency in English and martial arts ability, and looking the right age for the role.

Bordizzo had to put her university studies on hold for a while. She tried to combine her schooling with her acting job but eventually had to drop out.

How did Natasha Liu Bordizzo get into Hollywood?

Natasha Liu Bordizzo was cast in her first ever leading role in “Crouching Tiger, Hidden Dragon: Sword of Destiny” as Snow Vase. With no proper experience in acting but managed to ace the audition, although her first debut as “Snow Vase” required a lot of stunt work, for which Bordizzo even learned sword fighting, all this at just 19 years of age. After the release of her first film, she moved to Los Angeles to pursue an international acting career.

After gaining massive fame by playing the character of “Snow Vase,” Her next role was as Deng Yan in a highly acclaimed musical film, The Greatest Showman. Bordizzo played a Chinese acrobat along with stars Hugh Jackman and Zac Efron.

Her third feature film was a biopic Hotel Mumbai, about the tragic 2008 terror attack on the Taj Hotel in Mumbai. Her character was an Australian backpacker “Bree,” after “Hotel Mumbai,” Bordizzo featured in Netflix’s famous TV series called The Society; it is a mystery drama series where she plays the character of Helena she has primary role in it, and in the same year she did two films “Crazy About You” & “Guns Akimbo.”

She has also been a part of the short film Gong Shou Dao. She made a Chinese film debut with Detective Chinatown 2, a movie in Mandarin. Along with being a gifted actress, Bordizzo is a Chanel ambassador and has even modeled for Australian brand Bonds.
