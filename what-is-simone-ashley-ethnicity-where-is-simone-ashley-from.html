What is Simone Ashley ethnicity?

Simone traces her ethnicity to the Sri Lankan Tamil people. The Tamil people are a Dravidian ethno-linguistic group of people who trace their ancestry to India’s southern state of Tamil Nadu, union territory of Puducherry and to Sri Lanka. They constitute 5.9 percent of the population in India.
