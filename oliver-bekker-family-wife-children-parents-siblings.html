Oliver Bekker family, wife, children, parents, siblings

Oliver Bekker is a South African professional golfer who was born on December 11th, 1984. On the Sunshine Tour, where Bekker competes, he has triumphed seven times. He won the 2012 Dimension Data Pro-Am, the 2013 Telkom PGA Pro-Am, the 2016 Sun City Challenge, and the 2011 Northern Cape Classic. In 2017, he triumphed three times: in the Sun Wild Coast Sun Challenge, the Lombard Insurance Classic, and the Zambia Sugar Open.

At the Limpopo Championship in April 2021, Bekker lost in a four-man playoff. He came in second place at the SA Open Championship in December, trailing Daniel van Tonder by one stroke. He advanced to the European Tour for 2022 at the end of the season via the Challenge Tour standings.
