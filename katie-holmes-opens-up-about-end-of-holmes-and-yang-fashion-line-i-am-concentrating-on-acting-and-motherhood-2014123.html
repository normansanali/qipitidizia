Katie Holmes on Yang Fashion Split: Focusing on Acting, Motherhhood

No bad blood! Katie Holmes ended her five-year fashion line partnership with stylist Jeanne Yang earlier this month, but she had her reasons. The actress has opened up about the split in Elle UK, and revealed why she decided that now was the right time to cut ties.

"I loved every minute of my time working on Holmes & Yang. I remain passionate about the design aesthetic we created, and am so happy with what Jeanne and I accomplished," Holmes, 35, said in the mag's March 2014 issue. "However I am now concentrating on acting and motherhood, which did not leave the label the time and attention it deserved."
