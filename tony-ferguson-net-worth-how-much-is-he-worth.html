Tony Ferguson Net Worth: How Much Is He Worth?

Tony Ferguson is a retired American mixed martial artist who competed in the lightweight division of the Ultimate Fighting Championship (UFC). He was born on February 12, 1984, in Oxnard, California.

Ferguson had a successful amateur wrestling career before transitioning to mixed martial arts (MMA) in 2008. He made his professional MMA debut in 2008 and competed for various promotions before signing with the UFC in 2011.

During his UFC career, Ferguson had a long winning streak and became known for his unorthodox fighting style, his ability to absorb punishment, and his tenacity.
