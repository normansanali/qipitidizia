Lasercorn is an American YouTuber and social media influencer who is best known as one of the co-founders of the Smosh Games YouTube channel that has amassed more than 7.5 million subscribers. He also runs a personal, self-titled YouTube gaming channel that has also become a hit with his fans and has amassed more than 750k subscribers. Other than these 2 accounts, he has contributed to other channels as well and in 2017, he joined the entertainment-comedy YouTube channel Toaster Ghost. In 2014, he won the ‘Best Gaming Series’ award at the annual Streamy Awards ceremony. Lasercorn has developed a strong fanbase on other social media platforms as well with more than 350k followers on Twitter and more than 550k followers on Instagram.

    Lasercorn Facts

  • Before his rise to fame with Smosh Games, he used to host shows for Clevver Games, a subsidiary channel of Defy Media, one of the most widely-followed networks of YouTube channels. Before this, he had also worked as a lifeguard.
  • As a young child, he had wanted to be a race car driver. He later switched his ambitions after watching the 1993 science-fiction film Jurassic Park and dreamed of being an archaeologist.
  • His biggest pet peeve is being bombarded with modern millennial slang that keeps expanding and evolving all the time.
  • He revealed in an interview that he wished he had spelled his moniker Lasercorn with a ‘z’ instead of an ‘s’ as he felt that would have been much cooler.
  • From June 2014 to 2017, his hair was dyed orange. He had to do so after he lost a video game challenge against YouTuber and gamer Sohinki. The bet was that he would have to keep the dyed hair for a month but he kept it on for more than 2 years as he said that he was too lazy to remove the dye.
