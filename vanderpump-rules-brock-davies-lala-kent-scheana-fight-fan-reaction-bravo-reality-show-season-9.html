'Vanderpump Rules': Lala Kent doubts Brock, fans say 'she only sees others' red flags'

'Vanderpump Rules' Season 9 Episode 3 'Welcome to Rachella' saw the cast engage in a sport called 'Pickleball'. Tom Schwartz and his wife Katie argued about Tom Sandoval steamrolling him. Both the Toms talked it out with Schwartz chickening out of the situation as usual.

But the biggest event of the night was yet to come. James Kennedy finally popped the question to his longtime girlfriend! Yep, Kennedy and Raquel Leviss are now a newly engaged couple with the pair over the moon. 'Vanderpump Rules' follows the servers at Lisa Vanderpump's restaurant SUR as they make a name for themselves in the entertainment world, all while dishing plenty of drama.

Lala questions Brock's credibility as father

Last week's episode saw Scheana's fiance Brock drop a bomb casually. Lala who was hanging out with them got the disturbing news that Brock hadn't seen his two kids from a previous relationship for four years now. The nonchalant way Brock said it shook Lala Kent who is a new mother herself. In today's episode, we saw her express that concern in person. She addressed the issue first with Katie Maloney and talked about how Brock and Scheana acted like it was no big deal when in fact it is. Lala was worried about her friend and whether Brock was to be trusted.

Further into the episode while on a shopping expedition with Scheana for baby clothes she brought up the topic. She worriedly asked Scheana when did she find about Brock's tricky situation with his kids, Scheana told her he was honest right from the start and believes he won't leave their daughter. Lala retorts she hopes so and goes on to say if ever Scheana ever decided to cut ties with him that Brock would fight for Summer Moon (his newborn daughter). Although Scheana assures her Lala isn't convinced. The preview for the next episode shows a big fight and truth to be revealed.
