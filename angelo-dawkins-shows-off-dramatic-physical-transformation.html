Angelo Dawkins Shows Off Dramatic Physical Transformation

WWE Superstars can see big changes in their look throughout their careers. Sometimes that change isn’t incredible, but this time it is pretty impressive.

The Street Profits are getting a new wave of momentum in WWE as joined up with Bobby Lashley to form The Pride. Angelo Dawkins is certainly ready for the challenges that are ahead of him.

Angelo Dawkins is one WWE Superstar who let the company’s grind work for his benefit. He got in great shape, and the before and after photos are amazing to compare.

Dawkins posted an Instagram story to show off his progress. He was not even in horrible shape before, but the progress he made in tightening up and getting in great condition is very noticeable.

Karrion Kross and AOP’s new faction, the Final Testament, are now going against The Pride. That could be hard-hitting feud, and Angelo Dawkins seems ready to go to war.
