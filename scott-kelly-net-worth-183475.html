Scott Kelly Net Worth

Scott Kelly was born on February 21, 1964 in Orange, NJ. He is a decorated astronaut and a veteran of four space flights. He is best known for his role as Commander of Expedition 26 on board the International Space Station, and for piloting the Discovery Space Shuttle. He has received numerous awards and honors for his service, including the Legion of Merit and the NASA Space Flight Medal.

About

Commander of Expedition 26 on board the International Space Station. He piloted the Discovery Space Shuttle.

Family Life

He and his identical twin brother Mark Kelly were the first siblings to travel into space. He was married to Leslie S. Yandell until 2009. He later got engaged to Amiko Kauderer. He has two daughters, Samantha and Charlotte.
