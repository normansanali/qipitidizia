What size screws fit VESA mount?

Screw Sizes For Computer Monitor Mount

Most of today’s computer monitor and displays have either a 100 x 100 mm or 75 x 75 mm VESA square hole pattern. They require M4 screws for attachment of the mount to the display. This is the most common setup for monitors or displays that weigh under 30 lbs (14 kg).

How long should VESA screws be?

What are M6 screws?

M6 refers to a metric 6 mm screw. The outside diameter of the threads is 6 mm. The standard metric rack screw is actually an M6 x 0.1 mm. The number 0.1 mm refers to 0.1 threads per millimeter. You can identify an M6 screw by measuring the diameter at slightly more than 7/32″ (0.228″).
