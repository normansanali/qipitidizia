Lauren London Bra Size, Age, Weight, Height, Measurements

Biography:

Lauren London was born on December 5, 1984 in Los Angeles, California, U.S. Her first television appearance was in Everybody Hates Chris while her film debut was in ATL.

She went on to star in other movies including This Christmas as Melanie ‘Mel’ Whitfield, I Love You, Beth Cooper as Cameron “Cammy” Alcott, and in Baggage Claim as Sheree Moore. Other films include Next Day Air and Madea’s Big Happy Family.

In 2008, she landed a recurring role in the television series 90210 as Christina Worthy. As of 2013, she plays the role of Kiera Whitaker in the series The Game.
