Who is Eddie Hearn? | Fan Arch

Eddie Hearn, a prominent sports promoter, has made a name for himself in the world of boxing. Born in 1979, Eddie is the son of Barry Hearn, the founder of Matchroom Sport, one of the largest sports promotion companies globally. Eddie's journey in the industry began soon after leaving school when he started representing golfers for a different company before joining his father's company to promote online poker events.

Early Career and Entry into Boxing Promotion

Eddie Hearn's early work in the online poker industry led him to meet Audley Harrison, a heavyweight boxer who had left the ring after experiencing a decline in his career. Recognizing an opportunity, Eddie worked behind the scenes to arrange a boxing match between Harrison and David Haye, the heavyweight fighter who held the World Boxing Association (WBA) title at the time. Although Haye won the fight, it marked a turning point for Matchroom Sport, opening up new opportunities.

Success in Boxing Promotion

Eddie Hearn's success in promoting boxing events continued to grow with his involvement in the careers of various boxing champions. Some notable names include Anthony Joshua, Katie Taylor, and Gennady Golovkin. Eddie's ability to build relationships with boxers and participate actively in promotional events has contributed to his reputation as one of the world's top boxing promoters.

Growth of Matchroom Sport

Eddie Hearn's contributions have played a crucial role in the growth of Matchroom Sport. With his industry contacts and business acumen, Eddie has brought significant business to the company, helping it flourish even during challenging times when health regulations limited sporting events in the UK. His involvement has transformed Matchroom's boxing segment into a leading promoter worldwide.

Business Ventures and Ambitions

Apart from his success in sports promotion, Eddie Hearn has shown interest in attracting investors to Matchroom Sport. While his father, Barry Hearn, has encouraged keeping the business within the family, Eddie has expressed a desire to explore opportunities for external investment and has even considered taking the company public.

Leadership and Achievements

Eddie Hearn's leadership as the head of Matchroom's Boxing division has brought the company back to the forefront of the sport globally. Under his guidance, Matchroom Boxing secured a historic $1 billion deal with live streaming platform DAZN in 2018, staging 16 fights a year across America under Matchroom Boxing USA. In April 2021, following his father's appointment as President of the Matchroom Sport Group, Eddie took on the role of Chairman, overseeing Matchroom Boxing, Matchroom Boxing USA, Professional Darts Corporation, PGA EuroPro Tour, and Matchroom Media.

Personal Background and Influence

Eddie Hearn's upbringing and relationship with his father have had a significant impact on his career. Unlike his father, who grew up on a council estate, Eddie attended the exclusive Brentwood School. Barry Hearn, determined not to raise a privileged and soft-minded son, pushed Eddie hard and even engaged in a boxing match with him when Eddie turned 16. Despite their differences, Eddie's father continues to offer guidance and support in his career.

Eddie Hearn's journey from representing golfers to becoming a prominent boxing promoter showcases his determination, business acumen, and ability to build relationships with boxers. His contributions to Matchroom Sport and the growth of the company's boxing segment have solidified his reputation as one of the world's top sports promoters. With his ambitions to attract external investment and explore new opportunities, Eddie Hearn continues to shape the future of Matchroom Sport in the dynamic world of sports promotion.
