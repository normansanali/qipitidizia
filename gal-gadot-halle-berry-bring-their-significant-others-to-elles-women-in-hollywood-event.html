Gal Gadot &amp; Halle Berry Bring Their Significant Others To Elle's Women In Hollywood Event

Gal Gadot and Halle Berry hit the red carpet for ELLE’s 27th Annual Women In Hollywood Celebration held at Academy Museum of Motion Pictures on Tuesday (October 19) in Los Angeles.

The two actresses, who were being honored by the magazine, brought their significant others, Yaron Varsano and Van Hunt, to the celebration.

Moses Ingram, who was picked as the magazine’s Young Hollywood honorees, was also seen on the red carpet, next to Yvonne Orji, MJ Rodriguez, and Nicole Ari Parker.

Gal‘s Wonder Woman director, Patty Jenkins, also joined the party, too.

FYI: Gal wore a bright pink dress with Tiffany & Co. jewelry. Halle wore a paisley Dundas dress with a Jimmy Choo bag. Moses wore a lacey Christopher Kane look with a Jimmy Choo bag. Nicole wore Jimmy Choo shoes and bag. MJ also accessorized with a Jimmy Choo clutch.
