Who is Yael Yurman? - CelebNetWorth

It has been more than a year since the debut of Firefly Lane season 1 on Netflix. Fans are eagerly anticipating the arrival of season 2, scheduled for release on December 2. Before delving into the anticipated new season, a quick refresher on the talented cast and their other notable appearances might be helpful. For instance, did you know that Yael Yurman had a role in Once Upon a Time before her appearance in Firefly Lane? For more information about Yael Yurman and her career, continue scrolling below.

Who is Yael Yurman?

Yael Yurman, born on June 28, 2001, in Canada, is an actress recognized for her roles in “The Man in the High Castle” (2015) and “Once Upon a Time” (2011). At 21 years old, Yael, pronounced as “yai-el,” is a talented actress originating from Canada. While her professional portfolio might not be as extensive as some of the other cast members in Firefly Lane, her contributions are certainly noteworthy and impressive.

What’s her connection to Firefly Lane?

Yael Yurman is notably recognized for her role in Firefly Lane, embodying the character of Kate’s daughter, Marah. The series, adapted from Kristin Hannah’s novel, spans across several decades. It primarily chronicles the lives of two lifelong best friends, Tallulah—known as “Tully”—and Kate, beginning from their childhood on Firefly Lane.

Katherine Heigl portrays the adult Tully, while the teenage version of Tully is brought to life by Alissa Skovbye. Alissa Skovbye stars alongside Roan Curtis, who portrays the younger iteration of Sarah Chalke’s character, Kate.

What else has she starred in?

Yael Yurman achieved her breakthrough in 2017 by securing the role of Anastasia in Once Upon a Time. Following this, she had a minor part in Netflix’s “To All the Boys I’ve Loved Before.” Her career further expanded as she appeared in various TV shows, such as “Sacred Lies,” “The Man in the High Castle,” and “Snowpiercer.” Her most recent and current role is portraying Marah in the second season of Netflix’s “Firefly Lane.”
