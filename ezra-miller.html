Ezra Miller Height, Age, Girlfriend, Family, Biography, Net worth &amp; More

Ezra Miller (born September 30, 1992) is an American Actor and Singer from Hoboken, New Jersey. He started his career with the film Afterschool in 2008. Furthermore, the 26-year-old actor later appeared in City Island in 2009.

Miller also co-starred in Beware the Gonzo and Every Day. Moreover, he became famous after appearing in a movie titled “We Need to Talk About Kevin” as Kevin Khatchadourian. The film was a major hit and also became a Cannes Film Festival sensation. His full real birth name is Ezra Matthew Miller.

In addition, he later acted in Royal Pains as Tucker Bryant for two seasons. In 2012, Ezra also acted in The Perks of Being a Wallflower, a movie based on a novel with the same name. Miller also played one of the leads in Justice League. He played the character named Barry Allen / The Flash.

The actor praised for his role as Credence Barebone in the film Fantastic Beasts and Where to Find Them. In fact, the next part of a series called “Fantastic Beasts and The Crimes of Grindelwald” is all set to release in November 2018. He is playing a major role in the film opposite actress Claudia Kim (Nagini).

Profile, Parents & Early Life

The actor was born on September 30, 1992, in Wyckoff, New Jersey, United States. Therefore, Ezra Miller’s age is Twenty-six (26) years old, as of 2018. His mother’s name is Marta Koch.

His father Robert S. Miller worked as a senior vice president and managing director of Hyperion Books. Later, he became a publisher at Workman Publishing. The actor also has two older sisters Saiya and Caitlin.

Furthermore, Ezra’s ethnicity is mixed (Ashkenazi Jewish, German, Dutch). He attended Rockland Country Day School and The Hudson School. Later he dropout from his high school at the age of 16.
