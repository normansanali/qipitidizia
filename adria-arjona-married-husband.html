When Did Adria Arjona Get Married? Personal &amp; Career Facts

A year after engagement, Adria Arjona got married. She and her husband, besides having a fulfilling career and busy work schedules, manage time to relish each other’s company.

Let us know the facts about Adria Arjona’s married date and venue, also uncovering the details of the actress and her attorney husband’s career insights.

American actress Adria Arjona is popular playing the role of Dorothy Gale in the 2017 fantasy drama TV series, Emerald City. At age 20, she began pursuing her passion for acting as a profession in 2012.

When Did Adria Arjona Get Married?

After knowing each other for quite some time, Adria got married on 31 August 2019. As per wiki, she and her attorney husband, Edgaro Canales, age 37 exchanged the wedding vows in LA Antigua Guatemala at the Hotel Casa Santo Domingo. 

The adorable two got married, just a year after their engagement which took place in August 2018 per wiki. By profession, her husband has been a business affairs manager at Prime Video & Amazon Studios since June 2022.

Previously, he worked for STX Entertainment, William Morris Endeavor, and Movie Package Company. He started his career as an Attorney at the Canales Law Offices. As per LinkedIn, her husband went to Boston College Carroll School of Management to receive a Bachelor’s degree in Finance in 2009. Then, he got Juris Doctor certification in Intellectual Property Law from the University of Puerto Rico. 

Likewise, he has LLM in Entertainment and Media Law from the Southwestern Law School for a year until 2014. 

Career Facts & More

The 30-year-old has been in the acting world since 2012. At the age of 20, she pursued her career in acting and is known for some notable Movies and TV Shows. Some of her movie work credits include Father of the Bride (2022), Sweet Girl (2021), The Belko Experiment (2016), and Loss (2012) per wiki.

Besides movies, her TV Shows work credit includes Andor (2022), Monsterland (2020), Emerald City (2017), Narcos (2015), and Unforgettable (2014).

Adria Arjona Bio & Family: Wiki, Age, Height

The actress was born Adria Arjona Torres on 25 April 1992 in San Juan, Puerto Rico per wiki. Her family comprises parents Ricardo Arjona, age 59, and Leslie Torres. Adria’s father is a well-noted Guatemalan singer-songwriter. Likewise, her mother is of Puerto Rican descent.


She stands at a height of 5 feet 5 inches (165.1 cm) tall and weighs around 55 kg.
