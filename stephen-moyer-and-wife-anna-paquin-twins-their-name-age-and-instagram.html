Stephen Moyer And Wife Anna Paquin Twins: Their Name, Age And Instagram

Stephen Moyer and his wife Anna Paquin are parents to a set of fraternal twins from their decade-long marriage. Stay tuned to get to know more about his family below!

Stephen Moyer is the vampire Bill Compton portraying actor in the hit HBO series “True Blood.”

After that, he starred as Reed Strucker, the lead role in the Fox series, “The Gifted” from 2017 to 2019.

Moyer is active in both the film and TV industry and has been a part of quite some notable projects throughout his acting career.

Aside from acting, he also works as a director.

Meet Stephen Moyer And His Wife Anna Paquin

Stephen Moyer and his wife, Anna Paquin married on August 21, 2010, at a private residence in Malibu, California.

The couple had been in a relationship since 2007 since they first met on the sets of “True Blood.”

Anna portrayed the role of Sookie Stackhouse, Moyer’s love interest in “True Blood.”

Moyer and Anna were engaged in 2009 before tying the knot in 2010.

Anna is an active theatre, film, and TV actress just like her beau.

Who Are Stephen Moyer Twins? Know Their Name and Age

Stephen Moyer and his wife, Anna Paquin gave birth to fraternal twins from their marriage.

The twins include a boy and a girl whose names are Charlie and Poppy.

The Moyer twins’ age is 9 years old as of 2022. They were born in September 2012.

Charlie and Poppy are Moyer’s first set of children with his second wife, Anna.

He has a son named Billy, born to his first wife.

Likewise, the actor also fathers a young daughter, Lilac from his seven-year relationship with journalist Lorien Haynes

Follow Stephen Moyer Twins On Instagram

Stephen Moyer’s twin children are not on Instagram as they are too young to be on the internet.

However, their pictures are widely shared by Moyer and Anna on their respective Instagram handles.

Charlie and Poppy will be on the site when the time calls for.

On the other hand, Moyer’s adult children, Billy and Lilac are available on the site.
