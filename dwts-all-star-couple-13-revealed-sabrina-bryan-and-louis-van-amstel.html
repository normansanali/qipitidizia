DWTS All-Star Couple #13 Revealed: Sabrina Bryan and Louis Van Amstel

Fans of ‘Dancing with the Stars’ voted Sabrina Bryan in as the final celebrity to compete during the upcoming All-Star season on the hit ABC dance competition.

The news was revealed this morning on Good Morning America. Her dance partner will be veteran pro Louis Van Amstel.

Couple #13 will join a dozen other previously announced competitors to vie for the coveted mirrorball trophy. The majority of them finished in first or second place during previous seasons.

Bryan, of Cheetah Girls fame, was eliminated during week six of season five in what was described at the time as “the most shocking elimination” in the history of the show.
