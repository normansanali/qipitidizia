Peter Breck Net Worth, Income, Salary, Earnings, Biography

Joseph Peter Breck (March 13, 1929 – February 6, 2012) was an American character actor who appeared on stage, on television, and in films. He was born in New York City and raised in Los Angeles. Despite his role as the gambler and gunfighter John H. “Doc” Holliday on the ABC/Warner Brothers television series Maverick, rugged, dark-haired Breck is best known for his role as Nick Barkley, Victoria Barkley’s (Barbara Stanwyck) hot-tempered middle son in the popular 1960s ABC western The Big Valley. Breck is married and has two children.
