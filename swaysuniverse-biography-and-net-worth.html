SwaysUniverse Biography And Net Worth

SwaysUniverse is an alias for internet personality and YouTuber, Sway Calloway.

Sway Calloway is an American YouTuber, journalist, radio personality, executive producer, and rapper.

Sway Calloway was born on July 3, 1971, in Oakland, California, in the United States of America.

He is a product of Oakland High School in the United States. Enjoying rapping from his early days, he teamed up with DJ King Tech after high school, and the duo performed at various San Francisco Bay Area clubs.

Sway was the co-host of the nationally syndicated radio show, ‘The Wake Up Show’ as one half of the duo Sway & King Tech.

The channel, Sway’sUniverse, known for hosting music, news, and cultural programming, has over the years accumulated up to 2.34 million subscribers.

Sway now hosts “Sway in the Morning” on SiriusXM, Shade45 as well as the MTV show TRLAM.
