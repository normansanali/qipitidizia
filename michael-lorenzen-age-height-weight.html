Michael Lorenzen Age, Height, Weight

Michael Lorenzen Age, Height, Weight: Michael Lorenzen, officially known as Michael Clifton Lorenzen is an American professional baseball player.

He developed a passion for playing baseball at a younger age, has been consistent throughout his career, and has become one of the most sought-after baseball players.

At this time of writing (Sunday, August 13, 2023), Lorenzen is a professional baseball pitcher and outfielder for the Philadelphia Phillies of Major League Baseball.

He played college baseball as a pitcher and outfielder for the Cal State Fullerton Titans and was originally drafted in the 2010 MLB Draft by the Tampa Bay Rays

Later he was selected by the Cincinnati Reds in the 2013 MLB Draft. The Reds selected him in the first round of the 2013 MLB draft, making his MLB debut with the Reds in 2015.

On November 30, 2021, Lorenzen signed a one-year contract with the Los Angeles Angels. He went ahead to play for the team in the 2022 season, making his Angels debut on April 11, 2022.

At the end of his contract with the Angels, on December 20, 2022, Lorenzen signed a one-year contract with the Detroit Tigers as a free agent. He made his Tigers debut on April 15, 2023.

On August 1, 2023, Lorenzen was traded to the Philadelphia Phillies. He was traded to the Phillies prior to the 2023 season trade deadline. On August 9, he made his first home start.

The same day (August 9), he hit major headlines as he threw a no-hitter against the Washington Nationals. The no-hitter is the 14th in Phillies franchise history and the first since 2015.
