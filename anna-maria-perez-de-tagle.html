Anna Maria Perez de Tagle is an American actor and singer who made her theatrical film debut in Bee Season, a 2005 drama film adaptation of the 2000 novel of the same name by Myla Goldberg. Anna is also known for her roles like Ashley Dewitt in the teen sitcom Hannah Montana, Ella Pador in the films Camp Rock and Camp Rock 2: The Final Jam, Miracle Ross in the sitcom and how-to television series Cake, Safety Patrol Member Hero (voice) in the CGI-animated children’s television series Higglytown Heroes, Veronica in the Nickelodeon sitcom Just Jordan, Lainey in the fantasy drama series Charmed, and Jenna in the sitcom Baby Daddy.
